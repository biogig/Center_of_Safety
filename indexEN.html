Shedules

Road Safety

The training site is designed to practice the skill of safely crossing the roadway at a regulated and unregulated pedestrian crossing.

Details

Transport safety is one of the most important components state security, since, along with other infrastructure sectors, Transport provides the basic conditions for the life of society. To the population it is necessary to actively participate in ensuring transport security, and everyone a person individually must maintain law and order, strengthen his moral positions and respect the lives and rights of others. When providing transport security, it is necessary to take into account all possible threats from man-made, natural, terrorist and other nature.

Smart home

The site is built similarly to a smart home. All elements of the room operate on various sensors and can also be controlled remotely

Details

This is a smart home. A protected area, a smart mailbox, protecting children from the danger of falling out of windows, security systems against fire, burglary or gas leaks - the technologies of the future are increasingly entering our lives. The platform allows you to see the benefits of using smart home systems both in everyday life and in emergency situations.

Safety at home

This site uses augmented reality technology to search for possible hazards in residential areas

Detailss

At the site, using augmented reality technology, you can become familiar with the risk factors for fire caused by improper use of household appliances, learn the fire safety rules in a residential area, see the sources of potential threats and the causes of emergencies in everyday life.

All sites
Transport security

The main element of the site for practicing safety rules in transport is a life-size model of an electric train car

Details

Inside the carriage, using videos and virtual reality technology, the rules of behavior are studied not only in a railway carriage, but also in the subway, bus and airplane. To simulate emergency situations in the car, additional components are used that simulate the consequences of a frontal collision and smoke in the cabin. Visitors to the center are shown the importance of using seat belts, the absence of which can lead to serious consequences. Visitors to the center are also reminded of the rules for crossing railway tracks.

Rules of behavior in the forest

At the site, the rules for preparing for a hike in the forest, orienteering, safe lighting of fires, as well as behavior on reservoirs, etc. are practiced.

Details

Many people do not realize that the forest is a place of increased danger. Wild animals, ticks, snakes, and poisonous plants may lie in wait here. The most common danger is getting lost, or even ending up in a swamp. Since the beginning of the year, units of the Ministry of Emergency Situations have found more than 600 lost people in the forests, 29 of them children.

Tipping over car

The simulator allows you to demonstrate the possible consequences of a traffic accident with a rollover

Details

In this type of accident, the passenger moves by inertia, and it is possible that he will collide with parts of the vehicle interior or other passengers. Seat belts and child car seats can minimize these negative consequences

First aid

First aid is an important element in protecting the life and health of citizens. A person’s life may depend on the correct actions of eyewitnesses in the first seconds

Details

This exhibition is aimed at studying and practical testing of algorithms for providing first aid to victims. Depending on the age, various situations are played out - from insect bites, cuts to fractures, loss of consciousness and assistance with cardiopulmonary failure

Rules of behavior in a crowd

The playground allows you to feel the pressure of the crowd and practice the correct actions if you find yourself squeezed by a crowd, for children over 10 years old

Details

“crowd” is an unstructured accumulation of people devoid of a clearly perceived commonality of goals, but mutually connected by the similarity of their emotional state and a common object of attention. When small groups unite into a larger one, the likelihood of spontaneous behavior increases sharply. Important psychological characteristics of the crowd are its unconsciousness, instinctiveness and impulsiveness.

Earthquake safety

The training simulator presented on the site allows you to feel the impact of an earthquake with a magnitude from 1.0 to 9.0.

Details

The sudden collapse of a building occurs as a result of a natural or man-made emergency. A strong earthquake is impossible in our country, but on vacation abroad, children and adults should know how to act in such a case. The training simulator presented on the site allows you to feel the impact of an earthquake with a magnitude of up to 9.0.

About Center

Analysis of the consequences of various emergency situations shows that in more than 80% of cases the cause of death is the “human factor.”


Today there is no doubt that the solution to the problem of human safety largely depends on persistent preventive work, first of all, on educating the population in the basics of safe behavior.


The Ministry of Emergency Situations is carrying out targeted work to create educational safety centers in the Republic of Belarus.


To date, ten security centers of the Ministry of Emergency Situations have been created and are functioning in the republic: in the Gomel region (based on the Lyceum at the University of Civil Defense of the Ministry of Emergency Situations), Vitebsk, Lida (Grodno region), Brest, Stolin ( Brest region), Mogilev, Borisov (Minsk region), Baran (Vitebsk region), Novopolotsk (Vitebsk region) and Minsk.


The main purpose of the educational centers is to teach the population the correct actions in the event of various emergency situations. With the help of innovative technologies and simulators, visitors to the center have the opportunity to practice practical rescue and self-rescue skills. The expositions of security centers are built with maximum transmission of reality based on modern information technologies, using the digital environment, augmented and virtual reality technologies.


Training at the center is based on the principles of play. The basis is based on methods of immersing students in an environment that maximally simulates an emergency situation, and in-depth study of the material by increasing the emotional perception of the simulated situation. The center uses interactive trainers and simulators created on the basis of modern information communication technologies (virtual reality, augmented reality, Internet of things, computer vision, etc.).


The center features more than 30 interactive training platforms that allow training in the following areas:


  • transport security;
  • safety in emergency situations;
  • civil defense;
  • fire safety;
  • environmental safety;
  • security in ecosystems;
  • criminal security;
  • healthy lifestyle;
  • dangerous meteorological phenomena;
  • nuclear and radiation safety;
  • industrial safety.

The center allows you to organize training at a high level: practice actions in emergency situations, increase the level of life safety, and form the correct behavior. The skills acquired will help maintain health and reduce the loss of life.


At the end of the training, anyone can use specialized software to independently test their knowledge in the field of life safety and plan further training at the center.
